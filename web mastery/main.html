How to Add Your Name

Welcome to know me.com, a platform where you can showcase your profile and let people recognize you by your name.

When you submit your name, please note that the developer will upload it to the website only after two weeks. This process ensures that your details are verified and correctly displayed.

This service is completely free of charge. However, once your profile is successfully added, it cannot be updated or edited. Please make sure that you provide the correct information the first time.

If, for any reason, you wish to remove your profile, you may request removal only after a minimum of two months from the date of upload.
